Intrest, Amuse, Intstruct

A story is a narrative designed to interest, amuse or instruct the reader

Nelson Mandela told a story about what post-apartheid Africa could look like. That story was persuasive enough to promote change, and it became reality. JFK told a story about putting man on the Moon, and it inspired people and came to pass. <>

Some of the most successful companies in the world use stories. They use stories to better relate and sell their products and services. And chief storytelling officers design these stories, so what is a chief storytelling officer? <>

Tell engaging stories

Chief storytelling officers are responsible for telling engaging stories about the business. These stories typically include incorporating employee stories, enhancing brand awareness and personifying the brand and convincing people to engage with the brand.. Also aligning employees to the corporate journey <
